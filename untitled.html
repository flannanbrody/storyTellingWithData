We start at point X....where a hero (just a common person), crosses from the regular world which is above the line into strange supernatural world (which is everything below this line)

In that supernatural and strange world...at point Y....that hero encounters fabolous forces....and the hero has to fight an incredible battle.....to destroy those forces...in that strange world

Then having learnt the lessons from having fought that battle, the hero then at point Z, comes back into the regular world....but now a very changed person.....because of the battles that person had to fight in the strange world....when he comes back......he has this greater knowledge.
